Our Awesome Story

Check video presentation to find out more about us .

We are a bunch of explorers who believe that traveling is the best form of education. Discovering and experiencing new places is a pleasure one should enjoy in his / her life’s journey. We also believe that traveling need not be an expensive affair and with proper planning and looking out for bargain travel deal you can tick off very many touristic destinations off your bucket list. 

We are a Rwandan based holiday booking agency that covers Rwanda, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, Kenya and Central African countries. We strive to offer the best at pocket friendly prices while working with local teams to be able to offer unforgettable experiences to our customers

Tables and Hills is about finding the right holiday package for your travels, and discoveries. Something tailored to your taste with a little something more at an affordable cost and various payment options.

Our team is based all over Easter and Central Africa constantly scouting for the best deals for you. Book with us, travel with us. Let’s build memories!
